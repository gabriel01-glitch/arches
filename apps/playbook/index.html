Skills

Repeat jobs turned into one sentence rituals. They fire on their own when you use the right words. Tap a card to see what each one checks.

Fires on let's build, quiero hacer, what if we, se me ocurrio, or several ideas dumped at once.

  • Done means: one verifiable sentence, not "polished".
  • Smallest shippable version: what could be in a student's hands this week. Rest goes to a v2 parking lot.
  • Open tracks check: names your live tracks. Three or more open, it asks what this replaces.
  • 1 to 3 asset stop: the deliverable list maxes at three. Beyond that is scope creep.

Fires on deploy, sube, subelo, ship it, publish, or a premium build request.

  • Pre flight: audio baked as base64, iOS tap unlock present, ?name= works, fonts load, streak keys unique.
  • Deploy: save as index.html, drag into the existing Netlify site, never a new one.
  • Verify: open in incognito, tap test audio on the phone, run one full loop, open the link inside WhatsApp.
  • Then: warm Spanish handoff, and save the copy into the vault.

Fires whenever a price, rate, quote, or freebie appears, even if you do not ask.

  • Market anchor: what comparable work really charges, with a source.
  • Value to the buyer: priced on their outcome, not your hours.
  • Free work test: a gift you are choosing, or a price you are avoiding? Inner circle gifts are left alone.
  • One concrete move: a single number or sentence you can send.

Fires on review prompt, pasted AI feedback, or which AI should do this.

  • Generates pre filled review prompts, no brackets to fill.
  • Verifies legal, tax, and spec claims before applying any feedback.
  • Rejects unsourced claims. Gemini gets confirmation only.

Fires on la siguiente or any two week date range.

  • Periods run Saturday to Friday, twice. Default Mon 5, Tue 6, Wed 6, Thu 5, Fri 0 at $10/hr.
  • Preserves superscript ordinals, blue header cells, red subtotal.

Fires on caza, caza vuelos, caza ofertas, or any "is this a good price".

  • CUN to CDMX or AIFA primary, Orlando or Miami secondary.
  • Doters rule, INVEX Volaris basica card math, real total cost verdict.
